About Episteme

Get ready to witness the biggest cultural event of the year -
Episteme
Hosted by
QUBIT (BCAS Physics Society)

This annual fest is set to take place on March 29, 2023 , right at the heart of the college campus.

With a jam-packed schedule featuring thrilling races, brain-boggling quizzes, and insightful talks, Episteme promises to be a day filled with non-stop fun and entertainment.

So, come and join us as we celebrate the spirit of knowledge and creativity like never before!
